Lina Kostenko

(born 1930)

Lina Kostenko is a prominent Ukrainian poet, writer, and intellectual. She was one of the key figures of the sixties, a cultural movement that opposed Soviet oppression. Her poetry is filled with deep reflections on the fate of Ukraine, human dignity, and freedom. Her most famous works are "Marusya Churay", "Berestechko", and the collections "On the Banks of the Eternal River", "Uniqueness". Due to her refusal to cooperate with the soviet authorities, her books were not published for a long time. Despite this, Lina Kostenko maintained her independence and is still a moral authority in Ukrainian society
