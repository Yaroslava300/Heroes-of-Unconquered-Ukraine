Viktor Yushchenko

(born 1954)

Viktor Yushchenko is a Ukrainian politician, President of Ukraine (2005-2010). He became a symbol of the Orange Revolution, which took place due to the falsification of the 2004 elections. During the election campaign, he was poisoned with dioxins, but survived and continued the struggle. As president, he initiated democratic reforms, a course towards European integration and strengthening of national identity. One of his main achievements was the recognition of the Holodomor of 1932-1933 as genocide of the Ukrainian people
