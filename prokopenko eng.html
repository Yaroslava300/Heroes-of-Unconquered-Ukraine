Denys Prokopenko

(born 1991)

Denys Prokopenko is a Ukrainian military man, commander of the Azov regiment. He led the defense of Mariupol in 2022, became one of the leaders of the resistance at the Azovstal plant. After many months of defense, he was forced to surrender, but was later released as a result of an exchange. He currently continues to serve as the commander of the brigade. His resilience and courage made him a hero of Ukraine
