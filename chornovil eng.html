Vyacheslav Chornovil

(1937–1999)

Vyacheslav Chornovil is a Ukrainian politician, dissident, journalist, and one of the main fighters for the independence of Ukraine. During the Soviet era, he was persecuted for his views, spending many years in camps. He was one of the leaders of the People's Movement of Ukraine, which played an important role in gaining independence. Chornovil criticized corruption in power and called for democratic reforms. He died in 1999 in a car accident under mysterious circumstances. Many believe that it was a political assassination
