Yevhen Petrushevych

(1863–1940)

Yevhen Petrushevych is a politician, president of the Western Ukrainian People's Republic (ZUNR). After the collapse of Austria-Hungary, he headed the government of Galicia, trying to establish independence. He organized the struggle against Polish troops, but due to the defeat of the ZUNR, he was forced to emigrate. In Vienna, he continued his diplomatic activities, trying to attract the attention of the international community to the Ukrainian issue. He advocated the independence of Western Ukraine, but the great powers supported Poland. He died in 1940, without seeing the realization of his dream of an independent Ukraine
