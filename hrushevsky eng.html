Mykhailo Hrushevsky

(1866–1934)

Mykhailo Hrushevsky was an outstanding historian and politician, the first chairman of the Central Council of the UNR. His main work was "History of Ukraine-Rus", in which he argued for the historical separateness of Ukraine from russia. In 1917, he headed the Central Council, which proclaimed the independence of Ukraine. After the bolsheviks seized power, he emigrated, but later returned to the USSR. His scientific activities were under close supervision of the soviet authorities. He died in 1934 under unclear circumstances, presumably a victim of stalinist repressions
