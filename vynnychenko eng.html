Volodymyr Vynnychenko

(1880–1951)

Volodymyr Vynnychenko – political figure, writer, first head of the UNR government. He was one of the authors of the Fourth Universal, which proclaimed the independence of Ukraine. He advocated socialist ideas, which led to conflicts with Petliura and the bolsheviks. After the defeat of the UNR, he emigrated, engaged in literary activities and wrote about the political problems of Ukraine. He tried to return to the USSR, but did not agree to the conditions of the soviet government. He died in France in 1951, leaving a significant legacy in literature and politics
