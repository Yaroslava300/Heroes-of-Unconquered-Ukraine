Heroes of Unconquered Ukraine

17-18th century 18-19th century 1917-1921

The Heroes of Unconquered Ukraine are those who at different times defended the freedom of our state. From the Zaporozhian Cossacks to modern warriors, they sacrificed everything for the sake of independence. Their courage and devotion became the foundation on which our country is built. Every generation of Ukrainians has its heroes who prove: Ukraine is a land of free people. They inspire us to fight for the truth, preserve national dignity and remember the price of freedom. Their example reminds us that our struggle continues, and the future depends on each of us
