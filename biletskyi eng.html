Andriy Biletsky

(born 1979)

Andriy Biletsky is a Ukrainian military man, politician, founder of the "Azov" regiment. He took an active part in the war against the Russian occupiers since 2014, commanded volunteer units. Later he was engaged in political activities, created the National Corps party. At the moment he is the commander of the Third Separate Assault Corps. He is one of the symbols of the volunteer movement in Ukraine
