Ivan Franko

(1856–1916)

Ivan Franko is an outstanding Ukrainian writer, poet, playwright, public figure, and thinker. His work covers various genres, from political treatises to fairy tales. He wrote such classic works as "Zakhar Berkut", "Moses", "The Stonemasons", which became the basis of Ukrainian literature. Franko was an active fighter for the national independence of Ukraine and one of the first Ukrainian socialists. His activities contributed to the development of Ukrainian science, culture and politics
