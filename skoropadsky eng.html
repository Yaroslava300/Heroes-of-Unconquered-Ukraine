Pavlo Skoropadsky

(1873–1945)

Pavlo Skoropadsky – military and political figure, Hetman of the Ukrainian State in 1918. He came to power as a result of a coup supported by the Germans and established an authoritarian regime. During his reign, the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences was founded and the development of culture and education was supported. In December 1918, under pressure from the UNR Directory, he abdicated power and emigrated. In emigration, he developed the Ukrainian movement and headed the Ukrainian Union of Farmers-Statesmen. He died in Germany in 1945 during World War II
