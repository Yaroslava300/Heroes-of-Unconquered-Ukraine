On August 24, 1991, Ukraine declared its independence, which was confirmed by a referendum on December 1 of the same year. In 1996, the Constitution of Ukraine was adopted, which consolidated its status as a democratic state. The beginning of the 21st century was marked by the struggle for democracy, in particular during the Orange Revolution of 2004. In 2013-2014, the Revolution of Dignity led to the overthrow of the pro-Russian regime of viktor yanukovych. In 2014, russia annexed Crimea and began the war in Donbas. russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine on February 24, 2022 became the country's most difficult test. However, Ukrainians demonstrated heroic resistance, defending their independence. Today, Ukraine continues to fight for freedom, strengthens its army, and seeks integration into the EU and NATO
