Alina Mykhaylova

(born 1994)

Alina Mykhaylova is a Ukrainian volunteer, paramedic, and serviceman of the Armed Forces of Ukraine. Since the beginning of the war, she has actively helped the front, evacuating the wounded. She participated in battles as part of volunteer units. She is also a deputy of the Kyiv City Council. She was the fiancée of Dmytro Kotsiubaylo ("Da Vinci"). She continues to fight for Ukraine and help the military
