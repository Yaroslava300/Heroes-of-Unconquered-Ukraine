УКР

1917-1921

Petrushevych Hrushevsky Skoropadsky

This period went down in history as a time of struggle for Ukraine's independence from the russian Empire. In 1917, the Ukrainian People's Republic (UNR) was formed, headed by the Central Rada headed by Mykhailo Hrushevsky. In 1918, the Hetmanate of Pavlo Skoropadsky made an attempt to establish a strong state power, but his regime was overthrown by the Directory. The UNR fought a war for independence against bolshevik russia, Poland, and the white guards. The Western Ukrainian People's Republic (ZUNR) declared independence in 1918, but was captured by Poland. In 1919, the Act of Union of the UNR and ZUNR was signed, which symbolically united the Ukrainian lands. However, due to internal contradictions and the military superiority of the Bolsheviks, Ukrainian statehood was lost. In 1921, the Bolsheviks finally established control over Ukraine, incorporating it into the soviet union
