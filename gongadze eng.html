Heorhiy Gongadze

(1969–2000)

Heorhiy Gongadze was a Ukrainian journalist, founder of "Ukrainian Truth", who became a symbol of the struggle for freedom of speech. On September 16, 2000, he disappeared in Kyiv, and a few months later his body was found in the forest. The journalist's murder sparked the "Cassette Scandal," after which recordings emerged purporting to discuss his removal. The perpetrators of the murder were detained, but the instigators remain unknown. The Gongadze case became an important milestone for the Ukrainian press freedom movement
