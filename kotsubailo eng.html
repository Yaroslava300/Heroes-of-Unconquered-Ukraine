Dmytro Kotsiubaylo

(1995–2023)

Dmytro Kotsiubaylo was a Ukrainian military man, commander of the "Da Vinci Wolves" battalion. He participated in hostilities since 2014, becoming one of the youngest commanders on the front line. In 2021, he received the title of Hero of Ukraine, becoming the first volunteer to be awarded this title during his lifetime. He died on March 7, 2023 near Bakhmut, defending Ukraine from Russian occupiers
