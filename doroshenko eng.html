Petro Doroshenko

(1627-1698)

Petro Doroshenko was Hetman of Right-Bank Ukraine in 1665–1676. He sought to unite the Ukrainian lands under his rule and restore Cossack statehood. To do this, he entered into an alliance with the Ottoman Empire, hoping to receive military assistance. His army won significant victories, in particular over Polish troops. However, the alliance with the Turks caused discontent among the Cossacks and eventually Doroshenko was forced to renounce his mace. He ended his life in exile in muscovy
